About this Database

  • Explore English word families, compound words, definitions, and etymologies.
  • The British and American Word Families database supports vocabulary morphology instruction.
  • Morphology studies how words are built from meaningful units: bases, roots, prefixes, and suffixes.
  • Filter or search for words, morphemes, roots, and affixes.
  • Use sorting, filtering, and column search to find morphologically related words quickly.
  • Paste word + frequency into the Word Cloud tab to make visuals.
  • Use word families to build word walls.
  • Use Zipf frequencies to sort by commonness bands.
  • Download raw .csv.parquet files for offline use and analysis.

Column Definitions for the BAWF Morphological Database

Core Word Information

  • Word: The surface wordform.
  • Word_frq_Zipf: Zipf frequency score of the wordform.
  • FLemma: Lemma of the word.
  • GPT_Coded_Word_Parts: Morphological breakdown into prefixes, roots, and suffixes.

Baseword / Root Information

  • Baseword: Central base form after removing affixes.
  • Baseword_frq_Zipf: Zipf frequency score of the baseword.
  • Baseword_Family_Size: Number of forms in the baseword family.
  • Baseword_FamilySize_Incl_Compounds: Family size including compounds.
  • Baseword_Zipf_Inc_Compounds: Zipf frequency adjusted to include compounds.
  • GPT_Root: Primary root identified by GPT-assisted analysis.
  • Root_FamilySize: Number of forms sharing a root.
  • Root_FamilySize_Inc_Compounds: Number of forms sharing a root including compounds.

Greek / Latin Roots

  • Greek_Latin_Root_1: First classical root.
  • Greek_Latin_Meaning_1: Meaning of first classical root.
  • Greek_Latin_Root_2: Second classical root, if present.
  • Greek_Latin_Meaning_2: Meaning of second classical root.

Prefixes & Suffixes

  • Prefix_1 to Prefix_3: Prefix forms and meanings.
  • Suffix_1 to Suffix_6: Suffix forms and meanings.

Other Measures

  • N_Morphemes: Total number of morphemes in the word.
  • Reading AoA: Age of acquisition estimate.
  • Vocabulary eXpected Grade Level: Expected grade level.
  • GPT_Familiarity: GPT-estimated familiarity rating.
