About this Database

  • Explore English word families, compound words, and definitions/etymologies.
  • The British and American Word Families (BAWF) is a tool for vocabulary morphology instruction from the primary grades.
  • Morphology is the study of how words are built from meaningful units, including bases, roots, prefixes, and suffixes.
  • Filter or search for words you want and particular morphemes.
  • Use sorting, filtering, and column search to find morphologically related words quickly.
  • Paste word + frequency into the Word Cloud tab to make visuals.
  • Use word families to build word walls.
  • Use Zipf frequencies to sort by commonness bands (1st 1000 words, 2nd 1000, etc.).
  • Download raw `.csv.gz` files for offline use and advanced analysis.

Be inspired by joyful word learning and reading — dive in and explore!

Note: Large datasets may take several seconds to load the first time. After loading, filtering/sorting is instant in-browser.

Column Definitions for the BAWF Morphological Database

Core Word Information

  • Word: The surface wordform (as it appears in the dictionary and corpora)
  • Word_frq_Zipf: Zipf frequency score of the wordform (higher = more frequent)
  • FLemma: Lemma of the word (ignoring part of speech)
  • GPT_Coded_Word_Parts: Morphological breakdown into prefixes, root(s), suffixes

Baseword / Root Information

  • Baseword: Central base form after removing affixes
  • Baseword_frq_Zipf: Zipf frequency score of baseword
  • Baseword_Family_Size: Number of forms in baseword family (excluding compounds)
  • Baseword_FamilySize_Incl_Compounds: Family size including compounds
  • Baseword_Zipf_Inc_Compounds: Zipf frequency adjusted to include compounds

Root & Etymological Information

  • GPT_Root: Primary root identified by GPT-assisted analysis
  • Avg_Root_Zipf_BNC_COCA: Average Zipf frequency of words sharing root
  • Root_FamilySize: Number of forms sharing root (excluding compounds)
  • Root_FamilySize_Inc_Compounds: Number of forms sharing root (including compounds)
  • Avg_Root_Zipf_Inc_Compounds: Average Zipf including compounds

Greek / Latin Roots

  • Greek_Latin_Root_1: First classical root
  • Greek_Latin_Meaning_1: Meaning of first classical root
  • Greek_Latin_Root_1_FamilySize: Family size containing this root
  • Avg_Greek_Latin_Root_1_Zipf_BNC_COCA: Avg Zipf for words containing this root
  • Greek_Latin_Root_2: Second classical root (if present)
  • Greek_Latin_Meaning_2: Meaning of second classical root

Prefixes & Suffixes

  • Prefix_1 to Prefix_3 and Suffix_1 to Suffix_6: Include meaning, forms, frequency, count, and mean Zipf scores

Other Morphological & Psycholinguistic Measures

  • N_Morphemes: Total number of morphemes in the word
  • Reading AoA (Green et al., 2025): Age of acquisition
  • Vocabulary eXpected Grade Level (Flor et al., 2024): Expected U.S. grade level
  • GPT_Familiarity (Brysbaert et al., 2024): GPT-estimated familiarity rating
